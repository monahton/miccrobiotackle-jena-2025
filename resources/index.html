Knowledge Requiremnets

Some basic prior knowledge in R is needed:

We strongly recommend for those not yet familiar with R to take this opportunity and take these online tutorials below, since those are requirements for the workshop. This will help you to develop your programming skills and we can always learn a few tricks here and there, even if you are already experienced.

  • - R (part 1): https://swcarpentry.github.io/r-novice-inflammation/
  • - R (part 2): http://swcarpentry.github.io/r-novice-gapminder/

After taking those courses (or any other equivalent course in programming in R) will provide you with the basics in, for example:

  • - Files structure and Manipulation
  • - Data types and structure
  • - Loading, handling and manipulating vectors, matrices, factors and lists
  • - Manipulating and reshaping data
  • - and much more ...
