Day 1 Program

8:30am Registration and Welcoming!
Participants’ registration and welcoming to the start of the summer school.
We will also use this time to make sure all participants have the required software installed and to fix any installtions or setup issues.
9:00am Start - Introduction to the Workshop
Monah Abou Alezz - Theda Bartolomaeus
Introducing the instructors, outlining the code of conduct, and providing an overview of the summer school program.
9:30am Visualisation Theory
Monah
Overview of key visualization principles, including design theory, perceptual science, and best practices.
10:15am Introduction to Data Visualisation
Monah
A beginner-friendly introduction to data visualization tools and concepts. What are the different types of graphs and how to choose the best graphs for my data.
11:00am Coffee & Tea Break
11:20am Data Visualisation - Basics
Monah
Hands-on training and starting to work with Rstudio, focusing on the foundational aspects of creating basic visualizations.
1:00pm Lunch
2:00pm Data Visualisation - Basics (continued)
Monah/Theda
Exploration of basic visualizations and visualisation packages, expanding on earlier concepts with more complex datasets.
3:00pm Exercises and Applications
Monah / Theda
Interactive exercises that apply the visualization techniques learned throughout the day.
3:30pm Coffee & Tea Break
3:50pm Data Visualisation - Basics (continued)
Monah
Further practice and refinement of basic visualization techniques using R packages and additional datasets.
5:00pm Finish Day 1

Day 2 Program

9:00am Recap session
Monah Abou Alezz - Theda Bartolomaeus
A brief recap of Day 1, a quick recap and Q&A session addressing participant questions and solving any problems encountered.
9:30am Data Visualization: Advanced Customization Techniques
Monah
Explore advanced techniques for customizing data visualizations, focusing on improving clarity, aesthetics, and adaptability to different datasets.
10:15am Exercises
Monah / Theda
Hands-on practice to apply data visualization concepts and customization techniques covered earlier in the session.
11:00am Coffee & Tea Break
11:20am Data Visualization: Advanced Customization Techniques (Continued)
Monah
Further delve into customization strategies for complex datasets, covering tips to enhance communication through effective visual design.
1:00pm Lunch Break
2:00pm Creating Publication Figures
Monah/Theda
Learn the process of designing high-quality figures for publication, focusing on standards, tools, and techniques for visual clarity and impact.
3:00pm Exercises and Applications
Monah / Theda
Engage in practical exercises to reinforce the concepts of figure creation and data visualization, applying them to real-world examples.
3:30pm Coffee & Tea Break
3:50pm Wrap-up
Monah / Theda
Summarize the key takeaways from the summer school, followed by a Q&A session and discussion on the next steps and post-workshop survey.
5:00pm Finish Day 2
